## 4. Step-by-Step Account Creation Guide

Follow these steps in the Instagram app on your phone:

1.  **Create the Account:** Open Instagram, go to your profile, tap your username at the top, and select "Add account" -> "Create new account."
2.  **Enter Username:** Type in your chosen handle from Step 1.
3.  **Set Password:** Create a strong password.
4.  **Skip Contacts:** When asked to find Facebook friends or contacts, tap "Skip" (you want a fresh algorithm, not your personal friends).
5.  **Upload Profile Picture:** Upload the neon green crown/dollar sign logo I provided.
6.  **Switch to Professional Account:** This is the most important step.
    *   Go to your new profile.
    *   Tap the three lines (menu) in the top right.
    *   Tap "Settings and privacy."
    *   Scroll down to "Account type and tools."
    *   Tap "Switch to professional account."
    *   Select "Entrepreneur" or "Education" as your category.
    *   Choose "Business" (not Creator).
